THERE

Project Introduction

The brand of “Seeyou” is dedicated to the conservation of traditional culture. The branding team has collaborated to launch the “thousand layer sole hemp-rope embroidery project”. They have integrated academic research and the power of design and development to conserve this artistry and craftsmanship. They have investigated its sustainability and implemented multi development methods that not only enable re-design of traditional crafts, to make them more commercial, but also to create economic benefits that enrich the original local industry and revives rural communities, whilst taking into account cultural conservation. It also produces an innovative relationship between traditional artistic craftsmanship and contemporary designs that work hand in hand using each other as sources of reference.

 

Seeyou • There

“Seeyou” went back to the spot in Shanxi, where, according to historical data, the very first pair of rope-sewn cloth shoes was found. Beneath this ancient yellow soil, a wealth of primitive art forms and ancient folk arts were buried. “Seeyou” strives to search for remains of these rare and treasured cultural relics as well as any surviving veteran artists and craftsmen in the community The hope is that their ancient craftsmanship and skills could help to pass on their unsophisticated, honest and honorable philosophy of life to later generations.

 

The idea of “Seeyou” products is based on the concept of improving traditional craftsmanship and re-designing processes, leaving free space for creativity, as always, to local craftsmen, enabling them to fully express their understanding of life and their idyllic aspirational lifestyle.

 

Adhering to the above philosophy, “Seeyou” in Jin-Nan hereby presents to you ‘the thousand layers sole’ handcrafted cloth-shoe collection – “ Carrying on the Traditions”.
