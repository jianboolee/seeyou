Blossoming Joy

Seeyou • Blossoming Joy

In the current era of globalization, with the changes in lifestyle, habits and the religion of consumption, local traditional arts have been gradually losing their position. How do we revive the “old ways”? How can traditional folk arts find their place in a materialistic modern society? This requires more attention and constant research by more people.

 

In this design, the craftsman extracted the essence from the traditions of the art of paper cutting, then through a re-design process, experimentation with new materials, new media, new visual presentation methods, etc., attempted to explore the various possibilities to retain the traditional folk art. He attempted to build a bridge between the old and the new, whilst causing a wider audience to show interest and be curious about it.
