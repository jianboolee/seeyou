Brand Introduction

“Seeyou” is committed to creating modern derivatives of our intangible cultural heritage.

In this quest “Seeyou” is seeking to discover cultural relics and traditional artistry skills that are on the verge of disappearing and then will add contemporary design techniques to these re-discovered historical crafts. They are committed to channeling this ambition into creating everyday-objects with a story behind them.

 

Team Members

Our branding team consists of a group of young professional designers; they are passionate about traditional Chinese culture and are dedicated to the conservation and research of this traditional culture. They all hold Master’s degrees from the Guangzhou Academy of Fine Arts and are currently teaching either at the Academy or other, equivalent standard, institutions of higher learning.
